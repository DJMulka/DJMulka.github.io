About Me

I'm a Senior Software Developer with a strong foundation in electrical engineering and deep expertise in augmented reality, advanced sensing, and connected devices. I specialize in building intelligent, immersive systems that blend hardware and software to enhance industrial processes and streamline human-machine interaction.

My experience spans automotive manufacturing at General Motors, ADAS development at Delphi/Aptiv, and pioneering AR-driven automation at LightGuide Inc. This multidisciplinary background allows me to craft end-to-end solutions that integrate real-time sensor data, edge computing, and AR to guide operators, improve quality, and boost productivity.

I hold two pending patents in automation and have led the development of systems that have delivered multimillion-dollar cost savings, dramatic throughput gains, and improved product consistency. I'm driven to push the boundaries of connected industrial technology and excited to contribute to teams solving complex, forward-looking challenges.
