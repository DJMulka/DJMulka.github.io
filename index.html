About Me

I'm a Senior Software Developer with an electrical engineering background and a passion for building immersive, intelligent systems at the intersection of augmented reality, automation, and advanced sensing. Whether it's guiding factory operators with real-time AR overlays or integrating edge devices for smarter decision-making, I thrive on bringing connected technologies to life.

My experience spans automotive manufacturing at General Motors, ADAS development at Delphi/Aptiv, and pioneering AR-driven automation at LightGuide Inc. This multidisciplinary background enables me to build end-to-end solutions that have delivered multimillion-dollar cost savings, significant throughput gains, and improved product consistency. This work has resulted in a pending patent in automation software tooling.

Outside of work, I'm an avid skier, climber, and builder who loves to travel. I bring the same energy and curiosity to my career as I do to the outdoors: always exploring, always improving.
